Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 28, 2025

Data Collection

ContextGuard collects minimal data:

  • Optional: Email address if you choose to sign in with Firebase
  • User preferences (tone, reading level, target language)
  • No text content is stored or logged

Data Processing

Text you process is:

  • Sent to Google's Generative AI API for processing
  • Not stored on our servers
  • Not used for training AI models
  • Processed in-memory and discarded immediately

Chrome Extension

The Chrome extension:

  • Runs entirely client-side when using Chrome's built-in AI
  • Only requires permissions for page interaction
  • Does not track your browsing history

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your account and data at any time
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Opt-out of any data collection

Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at: privacy@contextguard.com
