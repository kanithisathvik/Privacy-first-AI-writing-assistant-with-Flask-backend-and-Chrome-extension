About ContextGuard

Our Mission

ContextGuard is dedicated to providing powerful AI writing assistance while respecting your privacy. We believe that advanced AI tools shouldn't come at the cost of your personal data.

Privacy-First Approach

Unlike other AI tools that send your data to external servers, ContextGuard processes text locally whenever possible using Chrome's built-in AI (Gemini Nano). When using our web app, we use secure, privacy-respecting processing.

Technology

Built with modern Python backend (Flask), Firebase authentication, and Google's Generative AI. The Chrome extension uses Manifest V3 and integrates with Chrome's built-in AI APIs.

Open Source

ContextGuard is open source and available on GitHub. We welcome contributions and feedback from the community.
